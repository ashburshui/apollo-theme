Reflection

It's a illusion to pursue perfect things, I emphasis that it's important to experience trivial things, such as chores. You will understand that in each kind of relationships, tolerance is the most important thing. We should never rely on this kind of "finding mindset", instead, we should put our hard work to make relationships extraordinary. And remember, unconditional love will teach one how to love himself, it comes before we learn how to give love to others. Shortage of love will lead to problems, and need efforts to pull back on right track.
